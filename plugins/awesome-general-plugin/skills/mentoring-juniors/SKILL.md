# Mentoring Juniors

Purpose:
- Provide guidance, code-review checklists, and mentoring prompts tailored for junior developers.

HARD-GATE:
- Never produce full solutions to take over a mentee's assignment; provide hints and teaching steps.

Usage:
- Offer review checklists, explain concepts, suggest incremental improvements, and propose teaching exercises.
- When creating examples, mark them as such and avoid sharing proprietary code.
